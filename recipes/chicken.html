Chicken Breast Recipe

I've made a few tweaks from the original recipe, but the spice combination used is one I've been using for a long time when making chicken.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • olive oil (enough to coat the chicken)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onino powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425F
  2. Mix together all the spices in a small bowl
  3. Add chicken breast to a larger bowl, and coat with olive oil
  4. Add spices to chicken breast and mix together
  5. Put chicken breast on baking sheet with parchment paper
  6. Cook until done(16-20mins. depending on size of chicken breast)
  7. Let chicken rest shortly, then cut into pieces and serve with side of choice
