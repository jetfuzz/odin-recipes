Chicken Breast

My go-to chicken breast recipe

My most used chicken recipe by far, and have used it many times.

Althought I usually make a few tweaks from the original recipe, the spice combination used is one I've been using for years when making chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • olive oil (idk how much just guess, enough to coat the chicken)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onino powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
